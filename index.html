1. This tool is not an official Roche tool.

2. This tool is to help reporting and verifying wfh/onsite ratio only, even if best effort is made to make it accurate, the creator can not be hold responsible in case of misuse or bad reporting or any consequences due to bad reporting.

3. This tool does not store or transfers any user data over any kind of network, all calculations are done locally in your browser, and deleted after usage.

4. You can use the copy button to copy the line you need and paste it in the official Roche google Sheet.

5. Only full hours are used, skeleton time is difficult to use, since days might be "hybrid", wfh+onsite and it is unknown to which entity to apply the deduction.

6. The sum used for ratio is (wfh+(onsite+remote)+travel), remote working hours in switzerland are considered as onsite.
